THEBOYZ

The Boyz is a South Korean boy group formed by Cre.ker Entertainment.
The group currently consists of 11 members:
Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, New, Q, Ju Haknyeon, Sunwoo and Eric.

The group debuted on December 6, 2017. Originally a 12-piece group,
former member Hwall officially left the group in October 2019 due to health reasons.
